Top Songs Analysis

The Best of the Best: Music Evolution Over Time

This is a data science project that explores how popular music has evolved from 1950 to the present, using Spotify's top 100 songs for each year (excluding 2024). Leveraging the Spotify API, the project examines key musical metrics such as Danceability, Beat, Genre, and Artist. By analyzing these trends over decades, the project aims to uncover patterns in music production and listener preferences, examining the dynamic shifts in the music landscape over time. Hopefully, we can gain a quantitative look into areas like why our parents claim ‘80s classic rock is the greatest music of all time’.
